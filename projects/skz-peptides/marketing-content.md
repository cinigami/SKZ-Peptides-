## Marketing Messages - Timeline Focus

### Quick Reference for Content:
- **Retatrutide:** "See appetite changes in 1-2 weeks, significant weight loss by month 2"
- **MOTS-c:** "Feel the endurance boost in your first week, peak performance by month 2"
- **SS-31:** "Energy improvements within days, full mitochondrial benefits in 2-4 months"
- **BPC-157:** "Pain relief starts in days, healing acceleration peaks in 4-8 weeks"
- **TB-500:** "Flexibility improvements in 2 weeks, maximum recovery benefits in 2-3 months"

### Content Strategy:
1. **Week 1 Posts:** Focus on immediate effects users will notice
2. **Month 1 Posts:** Highlight significant measurable changes
3. **Long-term Posts:** Discuss peak benefits and sustained results
4. **Comparison Posts:** Show timeline differences between peptides
5. **Expectation Management:** Set realistic timelines, avoid overpromising

### Threads Marketing Angles:
- "Day 7 vs Day 30 vs Day 90" progress comparison posts
- "What to expect in your first month" educational content
- Timeline infographics for each peptide
- User experience timelines (with proper disclaimers)
- "Fast vs slow-acting peptides" comparison content

- Timelines based on research data and user reports
