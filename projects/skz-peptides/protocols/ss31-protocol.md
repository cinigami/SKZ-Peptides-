## **🔬 Reconstitution Protocol**

### **Materials Needed:**
- 1x SS-31 10mg vial
- 2mL Bacteriostatic Water (BAC Water)
- Insulin syringes (29G-31G)
- Alcohol swabs
- Sterile work environment

### **Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Preparation**
   - Wash hands thoroughly
   - Clean work surface with alcohol
   - Allow vials to reach room temperature (10-15 minutes)

2. **Reconstitution**
   - Clean vial tops with alcohol swabs
   - Draw 2mL BAC Water into syringe
   - Inject BAC Water slowly down the side of vial
   - Gently swirl (do NOT shake vigorously)
   - Allow to dissolve completely (2-3 minutes)

3. **Final Concentration**
   - 10mg in 2mL = 5mg/mL (5000mcg/mL)
   - Each 0.1mL contains 0.5mg (500mcg)
   - Each 0.2mL contains 1mg (1000mcg)
